Project Overview

Bloom Care is a healthcare management app designed to streamline patient care, appointment scheduling, and health tracking. I implemented the entire frontend using Flutter, ensuring a smooth, responsive, and visually appealing user experience across mobile and web platforms.

Key features include real-time health monitoring, interactive dashboards, and secure patient data management. The app focuses on usability, accessibility, and performance.
